Rules of Danish

1 or Ace : you can attack with it

2 : you cancel a attack

3 : you copy the last card played

4 : just a 4

5 : just a 5

6 : just a 6

7 : the opponent must play with a card smaller than 7

8 : the opponent pass his turn

9 : just a 9

10 : you cut the discard, you can play any card after that

jack : just a jack

queen : just a queen

king : just a king
